# Alaska Air Annual Companion Fare Benefit  年度同伴票价福利



- Primary Cardholders whose accounts are open with active charging privileges earn a Companion Fare code after you spend $6,000 or more in Net Purchases that post to your Card billing statement during your Card anniversary year (every 12 months from the open date anniversary). With the exception of special account status or circumstances (e.g. Military relief programs), the anniversary will coincide with the month in which the annual fee is billed.
  开立账户并具有有效充值权限的主卡持卡人，在信用卡周年日（自开业周年日起每 12 个月）的净消费消费满 6,000 美元后，可获得 Companion 票价代码。除特殊账户状态或情况（例如军事救济计划）外，周年纪念日将与收取年费的月份一致。
- The Primary Cardholder will receive the Companion Fare code within the first 2 billing cycles after the open date anniversary each year, which will be credited to their Alaska Airlines Mileage Plan™ account. The code is valid for a round-trip or one-way coach Companion Fare on Alaska Airlines from $122 (USD) ($99 base fare plus taxes and fees, from $23 depending on your Alaska Airlines flight itinerary) when traveling with another guest on a paid published coach airfare on the same itinerary, booked at the same time on [alaskaair.comlink](https://www.alaskaair.com/).
  主要持卡人将在每年开放日期周年日后的前 2 个结算周期内收到 Companion 票价代码，该代码将记入其阿拉斯加航空里程计划 ™ 帐户。该代码适用于阿拉斯加航空公司的往返或单程长途汽车 Companion 票价，起价为 122 美元（基本票价为 99 美元，加税费，23 美元起，具体取决于您的阿拉斯加航空公司航班行程），前提是与另一位乘客乘坐同一行程的付费长途汽车机票旅行，并在 alaskaair.com 上同时预订。
- Multiple stopovers not allowed.
  不允许多次中途停留。
- Primary fare and Companion Fare must be purchased with the Primary Cardholder's Card.
  主要票价和双伴旅行票价必须使用主要持卡人卡购买。
- The Primary Cardholder can view and redeem the Companion Fare code by logging into their Mileage Plan™ account at [alaskaair.comlink](https://www.alaskaair.com/). The Companion Fare code must be redeemed within 12 months of the date of issue. Once ticketed, travel may be completed after the expiration date of the Companion Fare code. The expiration date cannot be extended and, once the Companion Fare code has expired, it cannot be exchanged.
  主要持卡人可以在 alaskaair.com 登录其里程计划 ™ 账户，查看和兑换 Companion 票价代码。旅伴票价代码必须在签发之日起 12 个月内兑换。一旦出票，旅行可以在旅伴票价代码的到期日期之后完成。有效期不能延长，一旦旅伴票价代码过期，就无法兑换。
- If your account is closed, you are no longer eligible to receive this benefit.
  如果您的账户被关闭，您将不再有资格获得此福利。
- Mileage cannot be used as a form of payment, however mileage credit accrual is allowed for both guests.
  里程不能用作付款方式，但允许两位乘客累积里程积分。
- The Companion Fare code is not valid with award travel, and cannot be combined with any other airfare discounts.
  双人旅行票价代码不适用于奖励旅行，也不能与任何其他机票折扣同时使用。
- Guest is responsible for all applicable taxes, fees, surcharges and applicable checked baggage fees. Applicable taxes and fees are subject to change.
  乘客负责所有适用的税费、费用、附加费和适用的托运行李费用。适用的税费和费用可能会发生变化。
- The Primary Cardholder can view their Companion Fare terms and conditions by logging into their Mileage Plan™ account.
  主要持卡人可以通过登录其里程计划 ™ 账户来查看其旅伴旅行票价条款和条件。
- The value of this Reward may constitute taxable income to you. We may issue an Internal Revenue Service Form 1099 (or other appropriate form) to you that reflects the value of such Reward. Please consult your tax advisor, as neither We, nor Our affiliates, provide tax advice.
  此奖励的价值可能构成您的应税收入。我们可能会向您发出反映此类奖励价值的美国国税局表格 1099（或其他适当的表格）。请咨询您的税务顾问，因为我们和我们的关联公司均不提供税务建议。

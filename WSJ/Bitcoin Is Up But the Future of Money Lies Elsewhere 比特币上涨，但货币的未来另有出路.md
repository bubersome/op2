# Bitcoin Is Up But the Future of Money Lies Elsewhere 比特币上涨，但货币的未来另有出路

## FOMO is no match for real utility, as an alternative digital gold rush in Brazil shows. 正如巴西的另类数字淘金热所示，FOMO 无法与真正的效用相提并论。

[Lionel Laurent](https://www.bloomberg.com/opinion/authors/ASpCEInTf5w/lionel-laurent)March 5, 2024 at 9:18 AM CST

Maybe he does. But very few others do.

*Photographer: David Ryder/Getty Images North America*
也许他做到了。但很少有人做到。摄影师：大卫·莱德/盖蒂图片社北美

A new form of digital money is going viral, capturing tens of millions of users, billions of transactions and the attention of global central banks. No, not Bitcoin, which climbed above $69,000 on Tuesday and set a fresh record for the first time since November 2021. Instead, I’m talking about Brazil’s cheap instant-payment system Pix. And the latter just might have a better claim to signposting the future than the cryptocurrency.
一种新的数字货币形式正在病毒式传播，吸引了数千万用户、数十亿笔交易以及全球央行的关注。不，不是比特币，比特币在周二攀升至 69,000 美元以上，并创下自 2021 年 11 月以来的新纪录。相反，我指的是巴西的廉价即时支付系统 Pix。而后者可能比加密货币更有资格指明未来。

Launched in 2020, the initial idea behind Pix — as with all good fintech solutions — was to boost financial inclusion, loosen the grip of a concentrated banking system and make payments quicker and more efficient. It’s succeeded at remarkable speed: It’s been used by 160 million people, or 80% of Brazil’s adult population, as well as 13 million firms, and now beats credit and debit cards as the country’s preferred payment method. The plan is to [take it global](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-02-27/brazil-takes-its-central-bank-digital-payments-app-pix-across-borders) at a time when central banks are experimenting with payment overhauls and digital currencies to keep a grip on tech disruption.
Pix 于 2020 年推出，其背后的最初想法与所有优秀的金融科技解决方案一样，都是为了促进金融普惠，放松集中银行体系的控制，并使支付变得更快、更高效。它以惊人的速度取得了成功：它已被 1.6 亿人（占巴西成年人口的 80%）和 1300 万家公司使用，现在已击败信用卡和借记卡，成为该国首选的支付方式。计划是在各国央行尝试支付大修和数字货币以控制技术中断时将其推向全球。

### The Other Digital Gold Rush 另一场数字淘金热

Brazil's instant-payments platform Pix is a hit at home and is going global
巴西的即时支付平台 Pix 在国内大受欢迎，并走向全球

Source: Brazil central bank
来源：巴西央行

Think of this as the flip side to Bitcoin’s “fear of missing out,” which is back with a vengeance after the approval of spot exchange-traded funds — essentially a shiny and more accessible wrapper for a token that’s still much more suited to hoarding and speculation than paying bills or generating predictable returns. Whereas the crowd-driven populism of Bitcoin is all about sticking two fingers at family, friends and financiers who caution against buying [what Warren Buffett dubbed](https://www.cnbc.com/2018/05/05/warren-buffett-says-bitcoin-is-probably-rat-poison-squared.html) “rat poison squared,” Pix is shamelessly institutional: It’s a fiat payment system run by the central bank; you need a bank account to use it and participation by commercial banks is mandatory. And there isn’t a blockchain in sight.
将此视为比特币“错失恐惧症”的反面，在现货交易所交易基金获得批准后，这种恐惧症又卷土重来了——本质上是对代币的一种光鲜且更容易获得的包装，而该代币仍然更适合于囤积和投机，而不是支付账单或产生可预测的回报。虽然比特币的由人群驱动的民粹主义完全在于对那些告诫不要购买沃伦·巴菲特称之为“老鼠药的平方”的家人、朋友和金融家竖起两根手指，但 Pix 却毫不掩饰地具有制度性：它是由中央银行运营的法定支付系统；您需要一个银行账户才能使用它，商业银行的参与是强制性的。而且看不到区块链。

Yet Pix is still futuristic in its own way, and doubtless healthier for the economy than the current buying of volatile tokens or memecoins like Dogecoin or Pepe in the hope that a greater fool will eventually come along and buy them back at a higher price. An International Monetary Fund study notes Pix transactions settle near-instantaneously and at a cheaper rate than other alternatives: It’s free for individuals and costs around 0.33% for merchants, which is lower than fees of around 1.13% for debit cards or 2.34% for credit cards — and lower than the 3.99% levy proposed by Meta Platforms Inc.’s WhatsApp payments system.
然而，Pix 仍然以其自身的方式具有未来感，并且毫无疑问比当前购买波动性代币或狗狗币或佩佩币等模因币更健康，因为希望最终会有更大的傻瓜出现并以更高的价格买回它们。国际货币基金组织的一项研究指出，Pix 交易几乎可以即时结算，并且比其他替代方案的成本更低：对于个人来说是免费的，对于商家来说成本约为 0.33%，低于借记卡约 1.13% 或信用卡 2.34% 的费用——并且低于 Meta Platforms Inc. 的 WhatsApp 支付系统提出的 3.99% 的征费。

There are, of course, risks here too, whether it’s the geyser of personal transaction data exposed to central-bank snooping, the risk of scams or the fact that the system has yet to be tested by a major financial crisis.
当然，这里也存在风险，无论是个人交易数据喷泉暴露给央行窥探、诈骗风险还是该系统尚未经受过重大金融危机考验的事实。

But you’d have to be truly blinded by digital gold to ignore the benefits of Pix. One study [cited by](https://www.thebanker.com/Brazil-s-PIX-payment-system-teaches-us-a-valuable-lesson-1677229744) The Banker found that the gains of this quick and convenient payment system have filtered down to small banks and local businesses away from big cities, resulting in more lending to entrepreneurs and households and more capital investment. Brazil’s central bank has also boosted digital literacy to ensure people don’t get left behind. In a world where transaction fees are still around 1% in many countries and weighing on growth, Pix seems like a genuine good-news story.
但你必须真正被数字黄金蒙蔽双眼，才会忽视 Pix 的好处。The Banker 引用的一项研究发现，这种快速便捷的支付系统的好处已经渗透到远离大城市的小银行和当地企业，从而导致对企业家和家庭的更多贷款以及更多的资本投资。巴西央行还提高了数字素养，以确保人们不会掉队。在许多国家交易费用仍约为 1% 并阻碍增长的世界中，Pix 似乎是一个真正的利好消息。

All of which takes us back to the less encouraging news of Bitcoin’s latest surge. Despite the sensationalism around ETFs, the irony is that there’s little new here: It’s the same old speculation, with the same old listed vehicles like MicroStrategy Inc. ready to do more buying (though there may be [revenue threats](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-01-15/coinbase-coin-at-center-of-bitcoin-btc-etf-machine-raises-concerns) for the likes of Coinbase Global Inc.). The risks that have bedeviled the crypto markets, though, are also still present, including worsening capital flight in emerging markets: Nigeria estimated $26 billion in untraceable crypto payments had passed through Binance’s local entity last year.
所有这些都让我们回到了比特币最新飙升的不太令人鼓舞的消息。尽管围绕 ETF 的轰动效应，但具有讽刺意味的是，这里几乎没有新东西：这是同样的旧投机，与 MicroStrategy Inc. 等同样的旧上市工具准备进行更多购买（尽管 Coinbase Global Inc. 等公司可能面临收入威胁）。然而，困扰加密货币市场的风险仍然存在，包括新兴市场的资本外逃加剧：尼日利亚估计去年有 260 亿美元的无法追踪的加密货币支付通过币安的当地实体流入。

Let’s hope Pix can prove over the long term that FOMO is no match for real utility — before the next crypto bubble pops.
让我们希望 Pix 能够长期证明 FOMO 无法与真正的效用相匹配——在下一个加密泡沫破灭之前。

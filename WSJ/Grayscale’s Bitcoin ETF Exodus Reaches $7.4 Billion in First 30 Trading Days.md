# Grayscale’s Bitcoin ETF Exodus Reaches $7.4 Billion in First 30 Trading Days

## GBTC’s 2024 outflow is second-largest among US-listed ETFs GBTC 的 2024 年流出规模在美股上市 ETF 中排名第二 Other spot Bitcoin ETFs have posted net inflows since launch 其他现货比特币 ETF 自推出以来已公布净流入

[Katie Greifeld](https://www.bloomberg.com/authors/AS35LxWAgr0/katie-greifeld)February 26, 2024 at 4:00 PM CST
2024 年 2 月 26 日下午 4:00 CST

The world’s biggest Bitcoin ETF has reached an inauspicious milestone: an entire month of consecutive outflows. 
全球最大的比特币 ETF 达到一个不祥的里程碑：连续一个月出现资金流出。

The $24.2 billion Grayscale Bitcoin Trust (ticker [GBTC](https://www.bloomberg.com/quote/GBTC:US)) has seen $7.4 billion exit across 31 trading days as of Monday, Bloomberg data show. GBTC has yet to post a single inflow since it converted into an ETF in mid-January.
彭博社数据显示，截至周一，规模达 242 亿美元的灰度比特币信托 (股票代码 GBTC) 在 31 个交易日内出现 74 亿美元资金流出。自 1 月中旬转换为 ETF 以来，GBTC 尚未公布任何资金流入。

That stands in contrast to the other nine freshly launched spot Bitcoin ETFs, all of which are sitting on net inflows since trading began. A few different factors explain the dichotomy: while GBTC is by far the largest and most actively traded of its peers, its 1.5% management fee means that it’s also the most [expensive](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-01-08/grayscale-s-1-5-fee-for-gbtc-would-be-highest-among-proposed-spot-bitcoin-etfs)offering, with the bulk of its peers charging below 0.3%. Additionally, bankrupt estates such as Genesis Global Holdco LLC have been looking to [offload](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/terminal/S8EOF9T0AFB4) their holdings. 
这与其他九只新推出的现货比特币 ETF 形成鲜明对比，自交易开始以来，所有这些 ETF 都处于净流入状态。有几个不同的因素可以解释这种差异：虽然 GBTC 是迄今为止规模最大、交易最活跃的同类产品，但其 1.5% 的管理费意味着它也是最昂贵的产品，而大部分同类产品的收费低于 0.3%。此外，Genesis Global Holdco LLC 等破产公司一直在寻求抛售其持股。

But even with those caveats — including that GBTC didn’t allow for redemptions in its prior structure — it’s surprising to see how consistent the outflows have been, according to Vident Asset Management. 
但 Vident Asset Management 表示，即使考虑到这些警告——包括 GBTC 在其先前结构中不允许赎回——看到资金外流如此持续也令人惊讶。

“Most of us in the industry expected to see outflows, we all knew it was coming — these long-term holders that were waiting for the fund to finally convert to get out,” said Amrita Nandakumar, president of Vident Asset Management. “Does it go to 60 days? I don’t think so, but then again, I’m surprised to see it got to thirty.”
Vident Asset Management 总裁 Amrita Nandakumar 表示：“我们行业中的大多数人都预计会出现资金外流，我们都知道这种情况即将发生——这些长期持有者一直在等待该基金最终转换以退出。”“是否会持续 60 天？我认为不会，但话又说回来，我很惊讶它已经持续了三十天。”



To be sure, outflows have slowed in recent days, with just $22 million leaving the fund on Monday versus as much as $640 million in January. But even still, GBTC’s $7.4 billion year-to-date outflow is the second largest among more than 3,400 US-listed ETFs.
可以肯定的是，最近几天资金外流有所放缓，周一仅有 2200 万美元资金流出该基金，而 1 月份资金外流高达 6.4 亿美元。但即便如此，GBTC 年内 74 亿美元的资金外流仍是 3400 多只美国上市 ETF 中第二大的资金外流。

“The Grayscale team anticipated GBTC’s diverse shareholder base would engage in profit-taking and deploy investment strategies that would impact the Trust’s flows, and we are pleased that outflows have continued to stabilize – especially against forced selling from estate bankruptcies,” a Grayscale spokesperson said in an email. “With market-leading liquidity, strong trading volume, and an unparalleled track record, we expect GBTC will continue to be a primary capital markets risk transfer tool for Bitcoin.”
“Grayscale 团队预计 GBTC 多元化的股东基础将参与获利了结，并部署会影响信托资金流的投资策略，我们很高兴资金外流继续稳定下来——尤其是针对破产遗产的强制抛售，”Grayscale 发言人在电子邮件中表示。“凭借市场领先的流动性、强劲的交易量和无与伦比的往绩记录，我们预计 GBTC 将继续成为比特币的主要资本市场风险转移工具。”

While GBTC continues to shed assets, the other nine spot Bitcoin ETFs that launched in January are pulling in billions amid the cryptocurrency’s rally. BlackRock and Fidelity are [leading](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2024-02-16/blackrock-ishares-bitcoin-etf-pulls-away-from-the-newborn-nine) the way, attracting about $6 billion and $4 billion a piece, followed by the likes of Ark Invest and Bitwise. 
尽管 GBTC 继续抛售资产，但 1 月份推出的其他九只现货比特币 ETF 在加密货币反弹中吸引了数十亿美元资金。贝莱德和富达领跑，各吸引了约 60 亿美元和 40 亿美元，紧随其后的是方舟投资和 Bitwise 等公司。

Still, GBTC has a loyal following among a population of cryptocurrency advocates for triumphing over the Securities and Exchange Commission. A judge’s ruling in August that the agency acted “[capriciously](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2023-08-29/us-court-paves-way-for-first-bitcoin-etf-in-grayscale-ruling)” in barring Grayscale’s conversion to an ETF was a key step leading to last month’s launches. It’s that brand of investor who would likely look past GBTC’s relatively high fee, according to Nandakumar.
尽管如此，GBTC 在加密货币倡导者群体中拥有忠实的追随者，因为他们战胜了证券交易委员会。8 月份，一位法官裁定该机构在禁止 Grayscale 转型为 ETF 时行为“反复无常”，这是导致上个月推出 ETF 的关键一步。Nandakumar 表示，根据品牌，这种投资者可能会忽略 GBTC 相对较高的费用。

“A non-traditional ETF investor who has a lot of brand loyalty to Grayscale, appreciate that they ‘stand for’ — standing up to the SEC and ‘getting it done,’ maybe they would be inclined to invest because they have a brand affinity to Grayscale,” Nandakumar said. 
Nandakumar 表示：“一位对 Grayscale 品牌非常忠诚的非传统 ETF 投资者，欣赏他们‘代表’——向 SEC 抗争并‘完成任务’，他们可能会倾向于投资，因为他们对 Grayscale 有品牌亲和力。”

The GBTC share price has jumped 40% so far this year, compared with an increase of around 34% for Bitcoin. The largest digital currency broke above $57,000 for the first time since late 2021 on Tuesday.
今年迄今为止，GBTC 股价已上涨 40%，而比特币上涨约 34%。周二，最大的数字货币首次突破 57,000 美元，创下 2021 年底以来的新高。

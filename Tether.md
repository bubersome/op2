

I suspect that it is difficult for the Crypto community to eliminate abuse through self-management. I have been thinking about what currency is recently. Perhaps politics, the rule of law, and the army are the basis of currency. Without these government foundations, all you have is a money transfer company. The reason Bitcoin is very popular in China is the capital control of the Chinese government. Why do I need a money transfer company in a place where capital flows freely?

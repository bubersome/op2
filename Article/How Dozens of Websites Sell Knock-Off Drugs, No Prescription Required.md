# How Dozens of Websites Sell Knock-Off Drugs, No Prescription Required

## Online sellers are advertising semaglutide and tirzepatide, the active ingredients in Ozempic, Wegovy and Mounjaro, at deep discounts with research-use disclaimers the FDA has called ‘bogus’ 网上卖家以大幅折扣宣传索马鲁肽和替泽帕肽（Ozempic、Wegovy 和 Mounjaro 中的活性成分），并附有被 FDA 称为“虚假”的研究用途免责声明

These websites are part of a robust online marketplace for what have been called weight-loss miracles, selling raw ingredients that cost far less than the brand-name drugs. They market to customers on social media, emphasizing discounts and “pharmaceutical grade” quality while stating that their products are “for research purposes” only. 
这些网站是所谓减肥奇迹的强大在线市场的一部分，销售的原材料价格远低于品牌药物。他们在社交媒体上向客户推销，强调折扣和“药品级”质量，同时声明他们的产品仅“用于研究目的”。

The Wall Street Journal identified more than 50 websites selling semaglutide and tirzepatide, the active ingredients in [Novo Nordisk](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/DK/XCSE/NOVO.B)’s Ozempic and Wegovy and [Eli Lilly](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/LLY)’s Mounjaro. Nearly all of them include disclaimers that the substances are “not for human consumption.” But several also include instructions for how to prepare human doses, or sponsor online forums where people explain how to inject the substances. The Food and Drug Administration has called similar websites’ research disclaimers “bogus” and says that customers shouldn’t buy their products, as they haven’t been tested by the agency.
《华尔街日报》发现有 50 多个网站销售索马鲁肽和替泽帕肽，这两种药物是诺和诺德 (Novo Nordisk) 的 Ozempic 和 Wegovy 以及礼来 (Eli Lilly) 的 Mounjaro 的活性成分。几乎所有这些都包含免责声明，即这些物质“不适合人类食用”。但其中一些还包括如何准备人体剂量的说明，或赞助在线论坛，让人们解释如何注射这些物质。美国食品和药物管理局称类似网站的研究免责声明是“虚假的”，并表示顾客不应该购买他们的产品，因为这些产品没有经过该机构的测试。

“Make HUGE GAINZ in your research this year,” said an Instagram ad from SAF Research, promoting a 70% discount on semaglutide and tirzepatide. “Pharmaceutical grade” available with “no prescription,” said an Instagram ad from Accelerate Labs for the two drugs. Zack Joseph, who runs Accelerate Labs, declined an interview request. A month’s supply of the ingredients costs around $100 to $200 on these sites, compared to as much as $1,350 a month without insurance for the brand-name drugs.
SAF Research 的 Instagram 广告称，“今年你的研究将获得巨大收益”，该广告宣传索马鲁肽和替泽帕肽 70% 的折扣。 Accelerate Labs 在 Instagram 上发布的这两种药物的广告称，“无需处方”即可获得“药品级”。 Accelerate Labs 的负责人扎克·约瑟夫 (Zack Joseph) 拒绝了采访请求。在这些网站上，每月的原料供应成本约为 100 至 200 美元，而没有品牌药物保险的情况下，每月的原料供应成本高达 1,350 美元。

SAF Research has advertised discounted semaglutide on Instagram.
SAF Research 在 Instagram 上打折索马鲁肽广告。

At least 18 of the sites have run ads on Instagram or [Facebook](https://www.wsj.com/market-data/quotes/META) in recent months, the Journal found, with some promoting “buy 1 get 2 free” deals. Most of the sites sell vials of the sterile water needed to turn the raw ingredients into injectable substances. At least 10 of the sites also sell syringes.
《华尔街日报》发现，近几个月来，至少有 18 个网站在 Instagram 或 Facebook 上投放了广告，其中一些网站宣传“买一送二”的优惠。大多数网站出售将原材料转化为注射物质所需的小瓶无菌水。其中至少有 10 个网站还销售注射器。

Novo Nordisk and Eli Lilly say they don’t supply ingredients to companies selling research substances and that their drugs are available only by prescription, to be taken in consultation with a doctor. None of the websites selling knockoffs includes the FDA warning that both semaglutide and tirzepatide increase risk of thyroid tumors.
诺和诺德和礼来公司表示，他们不向销售研究药物的公司提供成分，而且他们的药物只能通过处方购买，并在咨询医生后服用。销售仿冒品的网站均未包含 FDA 的警告，称索马鲁肽和替泽帕肽都会增加患甲状腺肿瘤的风险。

Widespread use of the unregulated versions of these drugs could quickly become a public-health issue, said Steven Lynn, former director of the FDA’s Drug Office of Manufacturing and Product Quality, as issues that arise for buyers may go unreported and unnoticed. 
FDA 药品制造和产品质量办公室前主任史蒂文·林恩 (Steven Lynn) 表示，这些药物不受监管的版本的广泛使用可能很快就会成为一个公共卫生问题，因为买家出现的问题可能不会被报告和注意到。

Meta Platforms, which owns Instagram and Facebook, removed ads for the sites and restricted or disabled some accounts after they were flagged by the Journal.
拥有 Instagram 和 Facebook 的 Meta Platforms 在被《华尔街日报》举报后，删除了这些网站的广告，并限制或禁用了一些帐户。

“Our policies prohibit the advertisement of prescription drugs without the proper authorization and approval, and when we identify violating ads we work quickly to remove them,” a Meta spokesman said. “We continue to improve our systems, including how we detect ads and behavior that go against our rules.”
Meta 发言人表示：“我们的政策禁止未经适当授权和批准就处方药做广告，当我们发现违规广告时，我们会迅速将其删除。” “我们不断改进我们的系统，包括如何检测违反我们规则的广告和行为。”

Laurie Cicotello, a 52-year-old from Denver, said she was prescribed Ozempic to treat her Type 2 diabetes before hitting an insurance coverage gap that made it difficult for her to afford. Cicotello, who suffers from lipedema, a chronic and painful condition that causes the buildup of fat in the body, said she watched her blood sugar levels fall out of the prediabetic range as she dropped from 450 to 375 pounds in six months. 
来自丹佛的 52 岁劳里·西科泰洛 (Laurie Cicotello) 表示，她被开了 Ozempic 来治疗她的 2 型糖尿病，但随后保险覆盖范围出现缺口，导致她难以承担费用。 Cicotello 患有脂肪水肿，这是一种导致体内脂肪堆积的慢性疼痛性疾病，她说她的血糖水平在六个月内从 450 磅下降到 375 磅，从而脱离了糖尿病前期范围。

“I was lamenting a lot, like ‘Wow, I finally found a drug that works to help me lose weight and now I can’t afford to take it,’” she said.
“我常常感叹，‘哇，我终于找到了一种可以帮助我减肥的药物，但现在我买不起了，’”她说。

A friend offered to order her “research only” semaglutide, and soon a month’s supply purchased at a much lower price arrived. After weeks of preparing her own syringes, Cicotello grew uncomfortable and went without any treatment for a few months. 
一位朋友提出订购她的“仅供研究”的索马鲁肽，很快以低得多的价格购买的一个月的供应量就到了。经过几周自己准备注射器后，西科泰洛变得不舒服，几个月来没有接受任何治疗。

“I don’t know what they’re putting in those vials, and that scares me,” said Cicotello. At the start of this year, when her insurance coverage kicked back in, she got back on Ozempic. Now she says she’s approaching another coverage gap.
“我不知道他们在这些小瓶里放了什么，这让我害怕，”西科泰洛说。今年年初，当她的保险范围恢复时，她又回到了 Ozempic。现在她说她正在接近另一个覆盖缺口。

The FDA received more than 3,700 reports of adverse events related to products containing semaglutide during the first five months of this year. None of them specify research use. The agency hasn’t verified that adverse events are caused by the drug.
今年前 5 个月，FDA 收到了 3,700 多份与含有索马鲁肽的产品相关的不良事件报告。他们都没有具体说明研究用途。该机构尚未证实不良事件是由该药物引起的。

America’s Poison Centers, which collects data for all 55 poison centers in the U.S., said there were 5,639 reports involving exposures to products containing semaglutide, tirzepatide and other GLP-1 drugs, from January 2021 to June 2023. The organization said it doesn’t distinguish between the brand-name drugs and those not approved by the FDA.
美国毒物中心收集了美国所有 55 个毒物中心的数据，该中心表示，从 2021 年 1 月到 2023 年 6 月，有 5,639 份报告涉及暴露于含有索马鲁肽、替泽帕肽和其他 GLP-1 药物的产品。该组织表示，它没有区分品牌药物和未经 FDA 批准的药物。

There are no FDA-approved generic versions of Ozempic or Mounjaro, and patents on the drugs are currently expected to last until at least 2032. 
Ozempic 或 Mounjaro 尚无 FDA 批准的仿制药，目前这些药物的专利预计至少会持续到 2032 年。

The online black market for drugs is huge, and has been since the early days of the internet. The FDA has issued dozens of warning letters to what the government calls “rogue online pharmacies” in recent years. Tens of thousands of such pharmacies have been identified in the past. The agency has prosecuted owners of websites using “research only” disclaimers to sell misbranded and unapproved drugs. 
在线毒品黑市规模巨大，自互联网诞生之初就一直如此。近年来，FDA 已向政府所谓的“流氓在线药店”发出了数十封警告信。过去已发现数以万计的此类药店。该机构已起诉使用“仅供研究”免责声明销售贴错标签和未经批准的药物的网站所有者。

Some of the sites selling knockoff semaglutide and tirzepatide appear to be run by fitness entrepreneurs and bodybuilders. The so-called research-grade steroids and peptides they sell are used by customers who want to appear stronger, leaner and younger. 
一些销售仿冒索马鲁肽和替西帕肽的网站似乎是由健身企业家和健美运动员运营的。他们销售的所谓研究级类固醇和肽是由那些想要显得更强壮、更苗条和更年轻的顾客使用的。

The site SAF Research previously styled itself “Swole AF,” which is short for “swole as f—” (or very muscular). Many of its social media ads have mentioned semaglutide and tirzepatide.
SAF Research 网站此前将自己命名为“Swole AF”，是“swole as f-”（或“非常肌肉”）的缩写。其许多社交媒体广告都提到了索马鲁肽和替泽帕肽。

An SAF marketing video on YouTube portrayed a bodybuilder bringing a wannabe musclehead to a gym he refers to as his lab. The bodybuilder touts “research chemicals by Swole AF Labs.” To the side is a rat in a cage. In online forums, “lab rat” is a euphemism for customers of research sites. “Our carefully crafted and unique blends of Research Materials allow you to push your research to the limits,” SAF says in customer emails. The YouTube video was removed after the Journal asked SAF about it.
YouTube 上的 SAF 营销视频描绘了一名健美运动员带着一名想成为肌肉发达的人来到他称之为实验室的健身房。这位健美运动员兜售“Swole AF 实验室的研究化学品”。旁边是一只关在笼子里的老鼠。在在线论坛中，“实验室老鼠”是研究网站客户的委婉说法。 “我们精心制作的独特研究材料混合物可以让您将研究推向极限，”SAF 在客户电子邮件中说道。在《华尔街日报》向 SAF 询问后，该 YouTube 视频被删除。

Business records and social media profiles show SAF is based in Southern California and run by Justin and Lana Brown. A spokesman for the Browns said that SAF has numerous disclaimers on its website and in its ads that its products are intended for lab research only. 
商业记录和社交媒体资料显示，SAF 总部位于南加州，由贾斯汀·布朗 (Justin Brown) 和拉娜·布朗 (Lana Brown) 经营。布朗夫妇的一位发言人表示，SAF 在其网站和广告中都有大量免责声明，表明其产品仅用于实验室研究。

Amy Johnson, an Ohio doctor and Type 1 diabetic, said she was looking for a low dose of semaglutide to improve her insulin sensitivity. As a hobbyist bodybuilder, she also hoped the drug would help her get six-pack abs. But her BMI of 19 meant she didn’t qualify for insurance coverage. 
俄亥俄州医生艾米·约翰逊 (Amy Johnson) 患有 1 型糖尿病，她说她正在寻找低剂量的索马鲁肽来提高她的胰岛素敏感性。作为一名健美爱好者，她也希望这种药物能帮助她获得六块腹肌。但她的 BMI 为 19，这意味着她没有资格获得保险。

Johnson ordered semaglutide from Peptide Sciences, another online seller of research chemicals. As a health professional, she felt she was taking a measured risk. But she said she quickly suffered severe side effects, including stomach paralysis that led to frequent vomiting, and stopped the medication within a few weeks. She believes it would be dangerous to use the drug without medical supervision.
约翰逊从另一家研究化学品在线销售商 Peptide Sciences 订购了索马鲁肽。作为一名健康专业人士，她觉得自己正在承担一定的风险。但她说，她很快就出现了严重的副作用，包括导致频繁呕吐的胃麻痹，并在几周内停止了用药。她认为在没有医疗监督的情况下使用这种药物是危险的。

“I see there’s a reason it shouldn’t be sold on the black market,” Johnson said. “It’s strange to me that I could find something like semaglutide so easily on the open web.” 
“我认为它不应该在黑市上出售是有原因的，”约翰逊说。 “对我来说很奇怪，我可以在开放网络上如此轻松地找到像索马鲁肽这样的东西。”

Peptide Sciences didn’t respond to a request for comment.
肽科学没有回应置评请求。

Meta’s policies state that only online pharmacies, telehealth providers and pharmaceutical manufacturers may run ads for prescription drugs. None of the sites reviewed by the Journal falls into these categories. After Meta took down ads that were flagged by the Journal, new, similar ads appeared on Instagram, some promoting the same websites. A Journal reporter was also served Instagram ads for websites selling anabolic steroids without a prescription, which is illegal in the United States.
Meta 的政策规定，只有在线药店、远程医疗提供商和药品制造商可以投放处方药广告。 《华尔街日报》所评论的网站均不属于这些类别。在 Meta 撤下《华尔街日报》标记的广告后，Instagram 上出现了新的类似广告，其中一些广告宣传的是相同的网站。 《华尔街日报》的一名记者还收到了 Instagram 广告，内容是销售无处方合成代谢类固醇的网站，这在美国是非法的。

Neil Spingarn runs a third-party testing lab that SAF has used to test some supplements it sells, according to letters posted on SAF’s website, but not its semaglutide or tirzepatide products. Spingarn said he won’t test injectable products because there are too many potential hazards, infections in particular.
根据 SAF 网站上发布的信件，Neil Spingarn 运营着一个第三方测试实验室，SAF 曾用该实验室测试其销售的一些补充剂，但不测试其索马鲁肽或替泽帕肽产品。斯宾加恩表示，他不会测试注射产品，因为存在太多潜在危险，尤其是感染。

Spingarn said many sites that claim to sell research chemicals operate under multiple names or websites. For regulators, he said, “it’s a whac-a-mole business.”
斯平加恩说，许多声称销售研究化学品的网站都以多个名称或网站运营。他说，对于监管机构来说，“这是一场打地鼠的生意。”

MZ Biolabs, a testing facility in Arizona, verifies the identity and purity of semaglutide and tirzepatide for a number of websites selling drugs billed for research only, including nine identified by the Journal. In an interview, the lab’s owners said they don’t recommend customers use those products based solely on its tests, as they don’t verify that vials are sterile.
MZ Biolabs 是亚利桑那州的一家测试机构，为许多销售仅供研究使用的药物的网站验证索马鲁肽和替西帕肽的身份和纯度，其中包括《华尔街日报》确定的九个网站。在接受采访时，该实验室的所有者表示，他们不建议客户仅根据其测试使用这些产品，因为他们无法验证小瓶是否无菌。

Ken Pendarvis, MZ’s analytical chemist, said people taking the research-only drugs risked “injecting bacteria directly into your bloodstream.” 
MZ 的分析化学家 Ken Pendarvis 表示，服用这些仅供研究使用的药物的人有“将细菌直接注入血液中”的风险。

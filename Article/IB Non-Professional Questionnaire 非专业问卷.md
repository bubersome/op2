IB Non-Professional Questionnaire 非专业问卷
Many exchanges employ a two tiered market data pricing structure based on whether the customer receiving the data is classified as a "nonprofessional" or "professional" recipient. The primary effect of this classification is the cost of the service for each subscriber status. Also, Professional customers must be reported to the exchange with detailed information including account title and location.
许多交易所根据接收数据的客户是否被归类为“非专业”或“专业”接收者，采用两级市场数据定价结构。这种分类的主要影响是每个订户状态的服务成本。此外，专业客户必须向交易所报告包括账户名称和位置在内的详细信息。


NON-PROFESSIONAL USER DEFINITION


A Non-Professional subscriber is an individual, who accesses information solely for the purpose of managing their personal or family investments. In addition, to be a non-professional subscriber, each of the following must be true:
非专业订户是指仅出于管理个人或家庭投资目的而访问信息的个人。此外，要成为非专业订户，必须满足以下各项：

The subscriber is not currently registered or qualified as a professional securities/futures trader with any national, state or international exchange, self-regulatory organizations or financial regulatory authority

The subscriber does not use the data for any commercial/business purposes
